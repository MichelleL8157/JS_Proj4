Grocery List

Record items and their prices onto the grocery list below. This table will help you organize all the necessary items to buy. There are several options to edit the table such as deleting items, adding items, and rearranging the order of priority of certain items.

Add Item

Enter the item name on the left text field and the item price on the right field. When you're done, press the "Add to List" button.

Switch Priority

If you want to change the order of the list, enter the number of the desired item according to its position from the top then click the up or down arrows to change its order. Remember to change the number in the text field if you want to change the order of one item as its position will change after every button or else it'll keep swapping with one other item.
